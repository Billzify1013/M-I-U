# MALAY INN UJJAIN - IMAGES GUIDE

## Image Requirements & Placement

**Location:** Place all images in the `images/` folder

### Required Images (MUST HAVE):

1. **hotel-hero.jpg** (1920x1080 or wider)
   - Main banner image for homepage
   - Best for: Hotel exterior, entrance, or scenic view

2. **hotel-about.jpg** (900x600 minimum)
   - About section image
   - Best for: Hotel interior, lobby, or ambiance shot

3. **deluxe-room.jpg** (1200x800 minimum)
   - Deluxe Room category image
   - Featured on homepage & rooms page

4. **deluxe-suite.jpg** (1200x800 minimum)
   - Deluxe Suite Room category image

5. **triple-bed.jpg** (1200x800 minimum)
   - Deluxe Triple Bed Room category image

6. **family-studio.jpg** (1200x800 minimum)
   - Family Studio Room category image

7. **family-suite.jpg** (1200x800 minimum)
   - Family Suite 3+1 category image

8. **premium-double.jpg** (1200x800 minimum)
   - Premium Double Bed category image

### Optional Gallery Images (For Background/Gallery Section):

- **lobby-1.jpg** through **lobby-3.jpg** (Hotel lobby & reception)
- **hotel-view-1.jpg** through **hotel-view-3.jpg** (Exterior/views)
- **amenities-1.jpg** through **amenities-3.jpg** (Hotel amenities)

---

## Quick Start After Adding Images:

1. Download this folder
2. Download images from Google Drive
3. Rename images as per the filenames above
4. Place them in the `images/` folder
5. Open `index.html` in your browser to preview
6. Upload to hosting/server
